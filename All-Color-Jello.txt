# All Color Jello
Recipe - All Color Jello. This was a staple at the table for Thanksgiving and Christmas meals. most visitors in the family had usually never seen or heard of it.

Found it also refered to as: Broken Glass Cake

## Ingredients

1 (3 ounce) package lime flavored Jell-O® mix
1 (3 ounce) package orange flavored Jell-O® mix
1 (3 ounce) package strawberry flavored Jell-O®

We have found that different flavors works just make sure to get contrasting colors

3 cups boiling water, divided
3 cups cold water, divided
1 cup canned pineapple juice
1 (.25 ounce) package unflavored gelatin
2 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
½ cup butter, melted
½ cup brown sugar
2 cups chilled heavy cream
½ cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

mix up the different flavor's of Jell-O according to the direction on the package. I like to use half the directed water to make a more firm jello piece.

# recieved from Mom

                                           All colored jello
You will need,
2- sm green jello
1-sm yellow jello
1-sm red jello
1 sm orange jello
A carton of whipping cream
 ( I think 12 or 15 oz, can’t remember the ounces on carton)
Graham crackers
Butter
Alittle sugar

Take your 4 jellos, put each flavor in pie tin add 1 cup of boiling water to each and stir till dissolved and let set up
The second green put in a bowl with cup boiling water, mix till dissolved, this one need to only be halfway set up you will add this to cream when you start whipping it.
Take your graham crackers and crush them to crust form, put in your dish add a little sugar and some melted butter (enough to make it crusty)
Take your fingers and press it in the bottom of your dish and up the side some, put aside.
Take your whipping cream add a pinch of vanilla and a little sugar if you want. Whip the snot out of it when starts to thicken add your green jello that is half way set up and finish whipping it
When your 4 jello’s are set cut them into squares and put them in your whipping cream and fold them together in the whipping cream, add that to the crust smooth it all out.
Sprinkle the top with some crushed graham crackers if desired.
